05.02.01 - 04 Foodsharing Copenhagen & storyboard & customer pitch

  • Focus

    Creating a campaign website for the grassroot Foodsharing Copenhagen. The page should address issues of food waste and/or inform and urge people to volunteer, through its copy, images and video content.

    Creating a storyboard for the video to plan camera angles and movements, shot lengths, transitions, props, duration and audio. Filmmaking using video and audio equipment.

    Pitching for customer - Foodsharing Copenhagen.

  • Process

    In a group of three, we wrote the treatment, developed the storyboard and made decisions for the webpage layout.

    My main roles were drawing the storyboard, location scouting, direct and shoot the video and write copy for the website.

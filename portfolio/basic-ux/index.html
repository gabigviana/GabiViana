03.01.01 data collection

  • Focus

    The assignment was composed of six different tasks for data collection, research and analysis. The focus was on building a data foundation to understand the user of a prospective design.

  • Process

    Desk research: collection of published information.

    Observation research: detached observation.

    Interview research: 12 minute recorded interview about chosen topic.

    Survey research: Google Forms and graphs questionnaire used as tools.

  • Learnings & Result

    UX research methods.

    Data analysis.

    Communication of research and test results.

    Documentation

03.03.01 prototype

  • Focus

    Build an interactive high-fidelity XD prototype of a website using a UI kit for helping new international students in Copenhagen (your target audience) find accommodation, a job and a social network.

    A digital solution that followed the common conventions in regard to design, content and functionality.

    UX oriented prototype with the intention of creating an optimal user experience for target audience.

  • Process

    Desk research on conventions regarding the design, content and functionality. Key points were stablished for visual communication and better user experience.

    Research for call of action on microcopy, specially for the sign-up form, which was a requirement for this assignment.

    Other important steps of the process: style tile design, Wireflow sketch, low-fidelity prototype, annd Think Aloud Test. The test gave some insights for change on the final result.

  • Learnings & Results

    Design conventions.

    UX focused digital communication.

    Desk research.

    Wireflow sketching.

    Think Aloud Test.

    Adobe XD prototypes.

    Documentation
    Prototype
